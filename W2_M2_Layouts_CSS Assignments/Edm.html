Electronic Dance Music a.k.a EDM

This page tells you all you need to know about EDM and why I think it is one of the best forms of music out there. Just to be clear, this is my opinion, I think all music is beautiful in its own way, and if listened to in the right mood, it can be mind-blowing. EDM is all about PLUR.

The beauty of EDM is the ability to incorporate different genres of music. In the below clip, we can see how different styles which range from

  • Pop
  • Rock
  • Alternative
  • Electronic
  • Dubstep
are mixed together to make a collection of sounds that just flows well together, to make your head bop.

Even though I do love the more pop-ish style, while studying or doing homework, I prefer to listen to the more upbeat genres such as Dubstep: 130 - 150 BPM and Drum and Bass (D&B): 150 - 180 BPM. I primarily listen to these styles while working out or at concerts.
